** Version control and backups
#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
(setq vc-follow-symlinks t)
(setq
   backup-by-copying t
   backup-directory-alist
    '(("." . "~/.emacs.d/backups"))
)
#+END_SRC

** No blinking cursor

Unfortunately, it's possible for the terminal to make it blink anyway.
#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
(blink-cursor-mode (- (*) (*) (*)))
(setq visible-cursor nil)
#+END_SRC
